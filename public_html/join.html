Join Our Team

Looking for an excellent preparation for law school? Or do you just want to strengthen
your logic and public speaking? These are a few of many reasons why joining UTMT could be your perfect solution.

Why should I join?

Mock trial is one of the best means by which to prepare for law school, and many of our competitors plan to seek a legal career. However, many others (including high-ranking members of the organization) do not have legal aspirations. Skills in acting, public speaking, logical argument, legal knowledge, etc. are all key to succeeding in mock trial. Thus, over the years, we have had many theater majors, philosophy majors, engineering majors, English majors, etc. compete and be very successful. When asking yourself whether or not you may want to try UT Mock Trial, the question is: would I enjoy this activity, and am I willing to put in the work to be successful? All other skills can and will be taught by your team captain. No background knowledge of the law or courtroom procedure is required, nor are you required to have competed in high school mock trial. The ability to speak clearly and articulately is perhaps the only prerequisite to joining UT Mock Trial; other than that, all UTK students are welcome and encouraged to try-out. Students may try-out as either a potential witness, a potential attorney, or both.

Ultimately, we cannot stress enough how prior knowledge is unnecessary to be selected for participation. Many of our most successful competitors came into the organization without any knowledge of the law or past experience. Evidentiary and courtroom procedure can be taught; natural talent cannot. Thus, we are looking for committed, dedicated individuals who wish to learn and grow from this experience.
