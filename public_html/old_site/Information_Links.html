Welcome members and those interested in joining the University of Tennessee Mock Trial Organization. This page is a portal to all things vitally important to our daily operation: team calendar (including tryout and tournament dates), team materials and most importantly our new organization collaborative file. This file is meant to be a work in progress. Essentially it is a remote place where all members may upload and download case files, ideas, information and other important documents/videos/voice memos. No worries, this will be password protected. Every member will be given the password by their captain. Please do not abuse this privilege! It is a great opportunity to keep lines of communication open between teams to make our organization a better one. Do not clutter the case file; if and when you upload do it in the proper folder. Rules for this new organizational tool will be discussed in team meetings at the beginning of the semester.
